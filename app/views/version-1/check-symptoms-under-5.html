111 online Check your young child's symptoms

We are testing a new symptom checker for young children aged 1 to 4 in some areas.

We will ask questions about some common childhood symptoms. You will not get a diagnosis but you will find out what to do next.

Who this service is for

This service is currently only for children aged 1 to 4 with:

  • a high temperature (fever)
  • breathing problems
  • a rash or skin problems
  • vomiting (being sick)
  • a cough
Information:

If your child has any other symptoms or you are worried about them, call 111 to speak to someone instead.

For children aged 5 and over use the main 111 online service.
