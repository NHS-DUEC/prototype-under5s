---
name: two-tone list
---

Used to highlight words at the start of sentences that aren't necessarily headings.

The highlight is normal text colour, but the rest of the sentence is slightly grey.

As used on the module zero page.

## Example

<ul class="nhsuk-list nhsuk-list--bullet app-two-tone-list">
  <li>
    <b class="app-two-tone-list__highlight">words&nbsp;</b>
    <br aria-hidden="true">
    that lead into other words
  </li>
  <li>
    <b class="app-two-tone-list__highlight">but aren't actually&nbsp;</b>
    <br aria-hidden="true">
    headings
  </li>
  <li>
    <b class="app-two-tone-list__highlight">they are merely&nbsp;</b>
    <br aria-hidden="true">
    highlighted words at the start of a sentence
  </li>
</ul>
